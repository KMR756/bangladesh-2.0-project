Pillars of "Bangladesh 2.0"

Innovation

Innovation for Bangladesh 2.0 is a movement to improve the country's digital infrastructure, economy, and social systems.

Sustainability

Sustainability for Bangladesh 2.0 is the idea of using sustainable practices to achieve economic growth without harming the environment.

Infrastructure

Infrastructure for Bangladesh 2.0 includes the development of physical and digital infrastructure, as well as social infrastructure.

Empowerment

Empowerment for Bangladesh 2.0 is a complex and multifaceted concept, encompassing various initiatives and strategies aimed at empowering individuals and communities across the country.

The Vision of Future

Bangladesh 2.0 aims to redefine the nation's trajectory by embracing cutting-edge technology, promoting inclusive growth, and ensuring sustainability. With initiatives in renewable energy, digital transformation, and robust infrastructure, the country is set to become a global model of development.

Digital Transformation

Creating a nationwide digital ecosystem to enhance connectivity and access to services for all citizens.

Green Energy

Investments in solar and wind energy to ensure a sustainable and eco-friendly future.

Modern Infrastructure

Building state-of-the-art infrastructure to support economic growth and global competitiveness.

Bangladesh Launches New Satellite

Date: 05/01/2025 Time: 10.25 PM

Bangladesh successfully launched its first satellite, Bangabandhu Satellite-1, in 2018. This marked a significant milestone for the country, making it the 57th nation to independently operate a satellite in space. The satellite is named after the nation's founder, Bangabandhu Sheikh Mujibur Rahman. It was launched by a SpaceX Falcon 9 rocket from the Kennedy Space Center in the USA.
